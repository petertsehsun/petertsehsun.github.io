About Myself

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Queen's University in the Software Analysis and Intelligence Lab (SAIL). I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia. I am working in the Performance Engineering team at BlackBerry. My current research focus is on improving the performance for Cloud applications that are implemented using ORM.

I also have great interests in Performance Engineering, Distributed Systems, Database, Data Mining, and Machine Learning. I am particularly interested in applying Static Analysis and Data Mining techniques to software systems to help software development.
